Commonly Asked Questions


Is your brand new-user friendly?

We noticed that people struggle with digital literacy, so we made our website to ensure that even those who aren't knowledgable in technology can navigate it! Simply click the options of the bar at the top of the website, and it will take you anywhere you need to go. Even the contents within those pages are made to guarantee you don't get confused since it guides you.


What is the purpose of your website

To make sure that people get to experience a website that prioritizes your convenience over anything. You simply just have to type in where you want to go in MRT Line 3 and you get to know, even with discounts applied.


What inspired you to make this website?

The representatives of our company have to attend to work, and that is only possible through commuting. Most of our staff take the MRT for convenience, however they have experienced difficulty in budgeting since they sometimes get confused in calculating for the overall price of commuting. Therefore, we were inspired to make a website that solves this issue many of the working class experience.
